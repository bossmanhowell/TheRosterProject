About Leroy & Company

Leroy and Company is run by owner Casey, her fiance Chris, and their vet tech roommate Jen. We are happy to work with you and your dog (or cat) to make sure we arrange the best possible experience for everyone! We can accommodate most "special needs" including dog aggression or leash reactivity. We can administer oral or injected medications. Between the three humans, there is more than enough love to go around! Dogs are typically only crated for 1-4 hours a day depending on Casey's dog training schedule.
